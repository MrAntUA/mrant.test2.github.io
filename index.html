Offer Building

We assist you from the thorough analysis of the tender documents until the submission of your offer to reach victory.

Depending on your own structure, resource network, skills and knowledge, a dedicated TASC consultant can supervise the response to the calls for tenders, take the lead or simply be available on demand.

The following services are available when customising your coaching:

  • Analyse calls for tenders’ documents
  • Structure tenders’ response documents
  • Draw up a list of required administrative  documents, approval’s, certificates, etc.
  • Create bid planning
  • Identify the desired requirements set by the Institution
  • Identify the key tender success factors
  • Identify complementary skills that are needed among third parties as partners or contractors
  • Create and negotiate agreements between you and these third parties
  • Complete offer’s layout
  • Analyse and edit the entire tender document before it is submitted
  • Delivering offer within the deadline

Partnership

We act as both an architect enabling partnerships to come together, and as an independent referee defining the rights, obligations, responsibilities and accountability of each partner.

We ensure new partners with strong research capabilities. We define the right partners according to the selection criteria. We provide you with the key documents to share with your future partners. We advise and ensure the right partnership through legal formal or informal structures.

We draw up the key documents/agreements (pre-bid agreement, bidding Memorandum of Understanding, partners’ tasks split, etc.) to cover:

  • Identifying the right partner(s) to build the most appropriate and winning Consortium
  • The roles and responsibilities of all parties involved.
  • How the structure will operate once the contract has been successfully awarded.
  • Building a network of similar (or not) identities to cover a larger territory
